  • Write one sentence that is definitely correct about the situation or event in the prompt.

  • Write one sentence that is definitely incorrect about the situation or event in the prompt.

Bonuses. We will ask four other workers to validate one out of every 50 sentences that we collect from these HITs. For every selected sentence that passes the validation phase, you will get bonus of $1, for up to $5. If you have more questions, please consult our FAQ.
